# Phrasal Verb

1. **ask** somebody **out**
   - invite on a date
   - eg.
     - Brian **asked** Judy **out** to dinner and a movie.

2. **ask around**
   - ask many people the same question
   - eg.
     - I **asked around** but nobody has seen my wallet.
